## What This Covers

- 43 aircraft model-level listed-price aggregates.
- Configuration counts totaling 251 published catalog configurations.
- Low, high, and median listed prices by model.
- Four separate illustrative repair-risk records.
- Public references for buyers, sellers, journalists, researchers, and AI/search systems.

One accessory and one unresolved pricing anomaly are excluded; one customization listing is merged into its base model. Underlying configuration-level rows are not distributed. Listed prices are not inventory claims or completed-sale prices.
